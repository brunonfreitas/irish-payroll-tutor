\# Chapter 01 - Introduction to Irish Payroll



\## Areas

PAYE, Revenue, Payroll Basics



\## Key Points

\- Payroll is the process of calculating and paying employees.

\- PAYE means Pay As You Earn.

\- Employers deduct tax from employees before paying wages.

\- Revenue is the Irish tax authority.



\## Morning Tips

\- PAYE means Pay As You Earn.

\- Irish employers must operate payroll through Revenue rules.

\- Payroll is not only paying wages; it includes tax, deductions and reporting.



\## Quiz Questions

Q: What does PAYE stand for?

A: Pay As You Earn



Q: Who is the Irish tax authority?

A: Revenue



Q: What is payroll responsible for?

A: Calculating pay, deductions, taxes and reporting.
