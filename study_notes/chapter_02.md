\# Chapter 02 - PAYE System



\## Areas

PAYE, Tax Credits, Tax Cut-Off Point



\## Key Points

\- PAYE collects income tax from employees through payroll.

\- Tax credits reduce the amount of tax payable.

\- The standard rate cut-off point decides how much income is taxed at the lower rate.



\## Morning Tips

\- Tax credits reduce the employee’s tax bill.

\- The standard rate cut-off point controls how much pay is taxed at the standard rate.

\- PAYE is calculated through payroll before the employee receives net pay.



\## Quiz Questions

Q: What do tax credits do?

A: They reduce the amount of tax payable.



Q: What does the standard rate cut-off point affect?

A: How much income is taxed at the standard rate.



Q: Is PAYE deducted before or after the employee receives net pay?

A: Before.
